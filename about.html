About Us

EventHub is a reputed event management agency based in Pune. Recognized among the top event planners in India, we specialize in organizing exceptional and tailor-made celebrations.

Our portfolio includes wedding planning, birthday parties, corporate events, destination weddings, rental services, and more. We are your one-stop destination for all kinds of events and celebrations.

With a team of trained, creative, and passionate professionals, Décor Destinations brings your vision to life through innovation, precision, and a personal touch. Every event is an opportunity to create lasting memories.
